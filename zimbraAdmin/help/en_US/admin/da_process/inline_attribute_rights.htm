Attribute Rights for Delegated Administration

Attribute rights are rights that allow a delegated administrator to modify or view (or not modify or view) a specific attribute on a target. The specific attribute being granted is configured on the target and the type of read (get) or write (set) permission is set. Attribute rights can be granted in any combination of attributes to grant positive or negative rights.

For example, the right to view the status of a domain would be configured as get.domain.zimraMailStatus. You would create an Access Control Entry (ACE) by selecting the Attribute Right as the type and domain as the target type, and type the attribute name as zimbraMailStatus.
