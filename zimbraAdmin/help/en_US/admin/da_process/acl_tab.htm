ACL page

The ACL (Access Control List) displays a list of Access Control Entries (ACE) granted ON the target you are viewing. You can add additional rights and edit or delete an ACE that is granted to this target.

Note: This is not a list of the rights that the target has been granted. To see a list of rights granted to the target you are viewing, click Configure Grants on the toolbar.
